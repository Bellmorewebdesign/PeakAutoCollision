About Us

Your neighborhood body shop on Merrick Road.

Peak Auto Collision is a local Bellmore auto body shop that cares about doing the job right. From walking you through the repair process to helping you deal with your insurance company, the goal is to take some of the stress out of a situation nobody wants to be in.
